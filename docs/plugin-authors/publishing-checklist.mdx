# Publishing Checklist

A short list to run through before you [submit a plugin](/docs/plugin-authors/submit-a-plugin), so your entry is accepted quickly:

- Your package is published to npm and installable.
- It exposes a valid [plugin manifest](/docs/plugin-authors/plugin-manifest).
- Your registry entry has an accurate `description`, the right `category`, and the `capabilities` your plugin actually implements.
- A `sourceRepo` link points at public, documented source.

This page will grow into a complete pre-submission checklist.
